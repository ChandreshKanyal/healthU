Medical Records

We offer comprehensive medical record storage, encompassing complete histories, reports, and prescriptions.

Doctor Recommendations

The application suggests suitable doctors for specific medical issues and identifies the nearest hospitals where those doctors are available, simplifying the decision-making process for patients.

Appointment Management

Users can book appointments and receive timely reminders for appointments and medication doses, ensuring proper healthcare adherence.

Blood Donation Alerts

The app can identify users with matching blood types who are in close proximity to a hospital in need of blood donations. It sends notifications to eligible users, optimizing blood donation efforts while preventing overcrowding at hospitals.
